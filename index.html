ⓘ About Really Good Trades

Your Seamless AFL trading card checklist.

Tracking your collection
Use the Own checkbox to mark cards you have. Use Wishlist to mark cards you're looking for.

Browsing
Click a subset row to expand it by team. Click a team row to see individual cards. Use Show all to skip team grouping.

Trade Finder
Scroll down and click Find best trades to see which other users have cards you want — and want cards you have.

Saving
Tick your boxes then click Save list to sync to the cloud. Your list is private and linked to your account.
